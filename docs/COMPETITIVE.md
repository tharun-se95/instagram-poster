*AutoShorts.ai and the broader AI content automation market.*

## AutoShorts.ai — Feature Summary

**Product**: Faceless video autopilot for TikTok + YouTube Shorts
**Model**: AI generates script + voiceover + stock visuals → posts daily

### Pricing (Confirmed from live site)
| Plan | Monthly | Yearly | Posts/Week | Watermark |
|------|---------|--------|-----------|-----------|
| Free | $0 | — | 1 total | ✅ |
| Starter | $19 | $133 (~$11/mo) | 3 | ❌ |

| Hardcore | $69 | $483 (~$40/mo) | 14 (2/day) | ❌ |

> ⚠️ **Only 1 active series on ALL plans** — major constraint for multi-niche creators.

### Their Strengths
- Zero effort after setup ("set and forget")
- 60s video generated in under 5 minutes
- Voice cloning (ElevenLabs integration)
- Direct API to YouTube + TikTok

### Their Weaknesses (Our Opportunities)
| Their Gap | Our Advantage |
|-----------|--------------|
| ❌ No Instagram Reels | ✅ Instagram is our primary platform |
| ❌ No personal photos | ✅ Your real photos = authentic brand |
| ❌ Only 1 series | ✅ Multi-niche batch support |
| ❌ Stock footage only | ✅ Personal moments = higher engagement |
| ❌ AI-invented content | ✅ AI curates YOUR life story |
| ❌ TikTok + YouTube only | ✅ Instagram + YouTube |

## Our Positioning

**We are NOT competing with AutoShorts directly** — we occupy a different white space:

> AutoShorts creates content from *nothing*.
> We create content from *your life*.

This matters because:
1. **Authenticity drives Instagram** — algorithm rewards genuine personal content over AI-generated stock footage
2. **Brand building**: personal photos create a recognizable identity; stock footage is interchangeable
3. **Future expansion**: our Phase 3 AI faceless videos will match AutoShorts on their own turf, but with a superior visual generation pipeline (Kling/Seedance over stock footage)
